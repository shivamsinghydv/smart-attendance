Smart Attendance System

Project Overview

The Smart Attendance System uses an ESP32 microcontroller paired with an RC522 RFID sensor to enable a fast and efficient way for students to mark their attendance. By simply tapping their RFID cards, students can register their presence instantly. This system not only saves time but also reduces the need for manual record keeping.

This solution is designed to be low-cost and accessible, making it ideal for educational institutions looking to upgrade their attendance tracking systems.
